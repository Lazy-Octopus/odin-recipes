Scalloped Potatoes


Scalloped potatoes in a creamy garlic sauce


A blend of mild herbs and strong garlic flavour give this dish a satysfing comfort food feel.


Ingredients


  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic fresh/paste/powdered
  • Cream
  • Dill
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Love

Steps:....

  1. Thinly slice potatoes to reduce cooking time
  2. Grease baking pan with olive oil and add potatoes
  3. Add cream and place in pre-heated oven at 375 fahrenheit
  4. Cook until fork penetrates softly through multiple layers of potatoe
  5. Add salt, pepper and dill to taste
  6. Plate and enjoy!
