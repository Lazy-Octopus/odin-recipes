Garlic Farfalle Pasta


Farfalle pasta with olive oil garlic dressing


A blend of mild herbs and strong garlic flavour give this dish a satysfing comfort food feel.


Ingredients


  • Farfalle Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic fresh/paste/powdered
  • Sundried tomatoes
  • Spinach
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt N' Peppa
  • In the house

Steps:....

  1. Bring pot of water to boil, add pasta, add salt, cook for 10-11 minutes for Al Dante
  2. Begin heating olive oil and and garlic in a saucepan
  3. After pasta is cooked to preference strain with cold water and set aside
  4. Finish sauce by adding sundried tomatoes and searing to a soft wilt
  5. Add salt, pepper and lemon juice to taste
  6. Plate and enjoy!
